Control4 SR-260 Remote Review: Complete Analysis

Model: Control4 SR-260

As authorized Control4 dealers, we've installed and programmed countless Control4 remotes for Chicago homeowners. What is a Control4 Remote?

Control4 offers several remote models, but they all share the same philosophy: seamless integration with your entire smart home. Unlike basic universal remotes, Control4 remotes are designed to work as part of a complete home automation system, controlling everything from entertainment to lighting to climate control.

Control4 Remote Models

  • SR-260: Classic button-based remote with LCD screen
  • NEEO Remote: Touchscreen remote with customizable interface
  • EA-1/EA-3/EA-5 Controllers: System controllers (not remotes, but work with remotes)
  • Control4 App: Free app that works as a remote on phones/tablets

Key Features That Make Control4 Remotes Stand Out

1. Seamless Integration

Control4 remotes don't just control devices - they're part of your home automation system. They communicate with Control4 controllers to coordinate multiple devices simultaneously. Press "Watch TV" and your TV turns on, lights dim, shades close, and audio switches - all automatically.

2. Beautiful Interface

Control4 remotes feature intuitive interfaces that are easy to navigate. The SR-260 has a clear LCD screen with icons, while the NEEO offers a full touchscreen experience. Both are designed to be user-friendly for the whole family.

3. RF Communication

Like higher-end URC remotes, Control4 remotes use RF (radio frequency), meaning you don't need line-of-sight. Control your home theater from the kitchen, adjust lights from the bedroom - it all works through walls.

4. Extensive Device Support

Control4 systems support thousands of devices:

  • All major TV and audio brands
  • Streaming devices (Roku, Apple TV, etc.)
  • Smart lighting (Lutron, Philips Hue, etc.)
  • Motorized shades
  • Climate control
  • Security systems
  • And much more

5. One-Button Activities

Control4's "Activities" feature lets you control multiple devices with a single button press. "Watch Movie" might turn on your projector, lower the screen, dim lights, close shades, and start your sound system - all from one button.

Battery Life: What to Expect

Battery life varies by model:

SR-260: Uses AA batteries, typically lasts 6-12 months with normal use. The LCD screen and backlit buttons are power-efficient.

NEEO Remote: Rechargeable battery, typically lasts 5-7 days with normal use. Includes charging dock for convenient charging.

Factors Affecting Battery Life:

  • RF usage: Controlling devices through walls uses more power
  • Screen brightness: Higher brightness drains battery faster (NEEO)
  • Backlight: Constant backlight reduces battery life
  • Frequency of use: More use = shorter battery life

Who Should Buy Control4 Remotes?

Control4 Remote vs. URC Remote

Many homeowners ask us: "Should I choose Control4 or URC?" Here's the honest comparison:

Control4 Advantages

  • More polished user interface
  • Better integration with Control4 ecosystem
  • More intuitive for non-technical users
  • Stronger dealer support network

URC Advantages

  • More customizable programming options
  • Better for complex custom setups
  • More flexible device integration
  • Often more cost-effective

Our recommendation: If you're investing in a Control4 system, use Control4 remotes. If you're building a custom system with URC, use URC remotes. Both are excellent - choose based on your overall system.

Common Questions About Control4 Remotes

Can I use Control4 remotes without a Control4 system?

No. Control4 remotes require a Control4 controller (EA-1, EA-3, or EA-5) to function. They're designed to work as part of the Control4 ecosystem.

How long do batteries last?

SR-260 uses AA batteries and typically lasts 6-12 months. NEEO has a rechargeable battery that lasts 5-7 days with normal use.

Can I program it myself?

Control4 remotes require professional programming using Control4's Composer software. This ensures everything works reliably and is customized to your home.

What if I lose the remote?

You can use the free Control4 app on your phone or tablet as a backup. You can also purchase additional remotes - Control4 systems support multiple remotes.

Our Verdict

Control4 remotes are excellent choices for homeowners with Control4 systems. They offer seamless integration, beautiful interfaces, and reliable performance. The one-button activities make complex operations simple, which is exactly what smart home automation should do.

However, they're not standalone products - you need a Control4 system. If you're investing in Control4 home automation, the remotes are a natural fit. If you're looking for a universal remote for a non-Control4 system, consider URC or other options.
