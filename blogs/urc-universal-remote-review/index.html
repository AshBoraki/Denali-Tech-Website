What is the URC Universal Remote (WATCH/HOME/LISTEN)?

This URC universal remote features prominent WATCH, HOME, and LISTEN activity buttons that make it easy to control different entertainment modes. It's designed for whole-home automation and can control TVs, audio systems, streaming devices, lights, shades, and more.

Key Specifications

  • Activity Buttons: WATCH, HOME, LISTEN for easy mode switching
  • Display: LCD screen (model dependent)
  • Battery: Rechargeable or AA batteries (model dependent)
  • Range: RF (Radio Frequency) and IR (Infrared)
  • Compatibility: Works with URC Total Control systems
  • Buttons: Backlit keys with clear labeling

Features That Make This Remote Stand Out

1. Activity-Based Control (WATCH/HOME/LISTEN)

The prominent WATCH, HOME, and LISTEN buttons make it incredibly easy to switch between different entertainment modes. Press WATCH to turn on your TV and video sources, LISTEN for audio-only, and HOME for general home control. This intuitive design means less button-pressing and more enjoyment.

2. Dual Communication (RF + IR)

This remote uses both RF (radio frequency) and IR (infrared). RF means you don't need line-of-sight - you can control devices from another room or even through walls. IR handles traditional devices that require direct line-of-sight. This combination gives you maximum flexibility.

3. Long Battery Life

Depending on the model, these remotes use either rechargeable batteries with docking stations or standard AA batteries. Rechargeable models typically last 7-10 days, while AA battery models can last 6-12 months. Either way, you won't be constantly changing batteries.

4. Extensive Device Control

This remote can control virtually everything in your smart home:

  • TVs, cable boxes, streaming devices
  • Audio systems (receivers, amplifiers, speakers)
  • Smart lights (Lutron, Philips Hue, etc.)
  • Motorized shades and blinds
  • Thermostats
  • Security systems
  • And much more

5. Customizable Activities

The WATCH, HOME, and LISTEN buttons can be programmed to control multiple devices simultaneously. Press WATCH and your TV turns on, cable box powers up, lights dim, and shades close - all automatically. This is what makes smart home automation truly smart.

Battery Life: What to Expect

Battery life depends on the specific model:

Rechargeable Models: Typically last 7-10 days with normal use. Heavy users might need to charge every 5-7 days. The docking station makes charging effortless - just place it down when done.

AA Battery Models: Can last 6-12 months with normal use. This is great for people who don't want to worry about charging.

Factors That Affect Battery Life:

  • RF usage: Using RF (controlling devices through walls) uses more power than IR
  • Backlight: Keeping button backlight on constantly reduces battery life
  • Screen brightness: Higher brightness drains battery faster (if model has screen)
  • Frequency of use: More use = shorter battery life

The good news: Whether rechargeable or AA batteries, these remotes are designed for long battery life. You won't be constantly changing or charging batteries.

Common Questions About URC Universal Remotes

Can I program it myself?

Technically, yes, but we don't recommend it. URC remotes require URC's programming software and significant technical knowledge. Professional programming ensures everything works perfectly and is customized to your specific setup.

How long does the battery last?

Rechargeable models typically last 7-10 days with normal use. AA battery models can last 6-12 months. Battery life depends on usage patterns and RF vs. IR usage.

What do WATCH, HOME, and LISTEN buttons do?

These are activity buttons that can be programmed to control multiple devices. WATCH typically controls video sources (TV, cable, streaming), LISTEN controls audio-only, and HOME controls general home automation. They can be customized to your preferences.

Can it control smart lights?

Yes! URC remotes can control Lutron, Philips Hue, and many other smart lighting systems when integrated with a URC Total Control system.
