What is the URC Universal Remote (WATCH/HOME/LISTEN)?

This URC universal remote features prominent WATCH, HOME, and LISTEN activity buttons that make it easy to control different entertainment modes. It's designed for whole-home automation and can control TVs, audio systems, streaming devices, lights, shades, and more.

Key Specifications

  • Design: Sleek button-based remote with modern aesthetic
  • Display: LCD screen for status and navigation
  • Battery: Rechargeable lithium-ion battery
  • Charging: Docking station included
  • Range: RF (Radio Frequency) and IR (Infrared)
  • Compatibility: Works with Control4 systems
  • Buttons: Backlit keys with clear labeling

Features That Make the Helo Stand Out

1. Sleek Button-Based Design

The Helo features a modern, sleek design with well-organized buttons that provide tactile feedback. Unlike touchscreen remotes, you can feel each button press, making it easier to use without looking. The button layout is intuitive and designed for whole-home control.

2. Dual Communication (RF + IR)

The Helo uses both RF (radio frequency) and IR (infrared). RF means you don't need line-of-sight - you can control devices from another room or even through walls. IR handles traditional devices that require direct line-of-sight. This combination gives you maximum flexibility.

3. Long Battery Life

The Helo uses a rechargeable battery with a docking station. With normal use, it typically lasts 7-10 days between charges. The docking station makes charging effortless - just place the remote down when you're done using it.

4. Extensive Device Control

The Helo can control virtually everything in your smart home through your Control4 system:

  • TVs, cable boxes, streaming devices
  • Audio systems (receivers, amplifiers, speakers)
  • Smart lights (Lutron, Philips Hue, etc.)
  • Motorized shades and blinds
  • Thermostats
  • Security systems
  • And much more

5. Seamless Control4 Integration

The Helo is designed to work seamlessly with your Control4 system. It communicates with Control4 controllers to coordinate multiple devices simultaneously. Press "Watch TV" and your TV turns on, lights dim, shades close, and audio switches - all automatically.

Battery Life: What to Expect

The Helo uses a rechargeable battery:

Real-World Battery Performance: With normal daily use (controlling TV, audio, lights, etc.), the Helo typically lasts 7-10 days between charges. Heavy users might need to charge every 5-7 days, while light users can go 10-14 days.

Factors That Affect Battery Life:

  • RF usage: Using RF (controlling devices through walls) uses more power than IR
  • Backlight: Keeping button backlight on constantly reduces battery life
  • Screen brightness: Higher brightness drains battery faster
  • Frequency of use: More use = shorter battery life

The good news: The docking station makes charging effortless. Most people just place the remote in the dock when they're done using it, so battery life rarely becomes an issue.

Helo vs. Helo Touch

Control4 offers both the Helo (button-based) and Helo Touch (touchscreen). Here's how they compare:

Helo Advantages

  • Tactile button feedback
  • Longer battery life
  • Easier to use without looking
  • More familiar button layout

Helo Touch Advantages

  • Modern touchscreen interface
  • Customizable layouts
  • More visual feedback
  • Sleeker design

Our recommendation: If you prefer buttons and want longer battery life, choose the Helo. If you want a modern touchscreen experience, choose the Helo Touch.

Common Questions About the Helo

Can I use the Helo without a Control4 system?

No. The Helo requires a Control4 controller (EA-1, EA-3, or EA-5) to function. It's designed to work as part of the Control4 ecosystem.

How long does the battery last?

The Helo typically lasts 7-10 days with normal use. The docking station makes charging effortless - just place it down when done.

Can I program it myself?

The Helo requires professional programming using Control4's Composer software. This ensures everything works perfectly and is customized to your home.

Can it control smart lights?

Yes! The Helo can control Lutron, Philips Hue, and many other smart lighting systems when integrated with a Control4 system.
