Popular Smart Home Features in Arlington Heights

Based on what we've installed in Arlington Heights homes, here are the most popular smart home features:

1. Smart Lighting Control

Arlington Heights homeowners love smart lighting because it's one of the most visible improvements. Smart lights allow you to:

  • Control lights from your phone or voice (Alexa, Google Assistant)
  • Create scenes (e.g., "Goodnight" turns off all lights and locks doors)
  • Automate based on time of day or motion sensors
  • Save energy with automated scheduling
  • Enhance security with lights that turn on when you're away

Popular in Arlington Heights: Lutron smart dimmers and switches are the most requested. They're reliable, work with all smart home systems, and integrate seamlessly with Control4 and URC.

Cost in Arlington Heights: $2,000-$8,000 depending on number of switches. A typical Arlington Heights home needs 15-25 switches.

2. Security & Surveillance Systems

Security is a top priority for Arlington Heights families. Smart security systems include:

  • 4K security cameras (indoor and outdoor)
  • Video doorbells (Ring, Nest, or integrated systems)
  • Smart locks (keyless entry, remote locking)
  • Motion sensors and door/window sensors
  • Professional monitoring and mobile app access

Why it matters in Arlington Heights: While Arlington Heights is a safe community, smart security provides peace of mind, especially when traveling or at work. Many Arlington Heights homeowners value being able to check their home from anywhere.

Cost in Arlington Heights: $2,000-$10,000 for a complete security system with 4-8 cameras.

3. Motorized Shades & Window Treatments

Motorized shades are incredibly popular in Arlington Heights homes, especially for:

  • Large windows in living rooms and great rooms
  • Bedrooms for automated wake-up routines
  • Energy efficiency (close during hot summer days, open for natural light)
  • Privacy control with a button press
  • Integration with smart home scenes

Popular brands in Arlington Heights: Hunter Douglas and Lutron motorized shades are the most requested. They're reliable, quiet, and integrate perfectly with Control4 and URC systems.

Cost in Arlington Heights: $3,000-$12,000 depending on number of windows. Typical Arlington Heights home has 10-20 windows.

4. Whole Home Audio

Music throughout your Arlington Heights home is a game-changer. Whole home audio includes:

  • In-wall or in-ceiling speakers in multiple rooms
  • Sonos systems or distributed audio
  • Control from phone, tablet, or voice
  • Different music in different rooms, or synchronized throughout
  • Outdoor speakers for patios and decks

Popular in Arlington Heights: Sonos systems are very popular for their ease of use and wireless capabilities. For premium installations, distributed audio systems provide professional-grade sound throughout the home.

Cost in Arlington Heights: $5,000-$20,000 depending on number of zones and speaker quality.

5. Home Theater Systems

Arlington Heights homeowners love creating dedicated entertainment spaces. Home theaters include:

  • 4K laser projectors or large-screen TVs
  • Dolby Atmos surround sound systems
  • Acoustic treatment for optimal sound
  • Smart lighting control (dim lights when movie starts)
  • Comfortable seating and room design

Perfect for: Basements, bonus rooms, or dedicated media rooms in Arlington Heights homes. Many Arlington Heights homeowners convert unused space into impressive home theaters.

Cost in Arlington Heights: $15,000-$100,000+ depending on size and equipment quality. Entry-level systems start around $15,000.

6. WiFi & Network Solutions

Strong WiFi is essential for smart homes. Arlington Heights homes often need:

  • Mesh WiFi systems for whole-home coverage
  • WiFi 6 or WiFi 6E for faster speeds
  • Elimination of dead zones throughout the home
  • Support for 20+ connected devices

Why it matters: Many Arlington Heights homes are larger or have multiple floors, which can create WiFi dead zones. Professional mesh WiFi installation ensures perfect coverage everywhere.

Cost in Arlington Heights: $2,000-$5,000 for professional mesh WiFi installation.
