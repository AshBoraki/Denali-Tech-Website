What is the Control4 Helo Touch?

The Control4 Helo Touch is a higher-end touchscreen remote control designed for whole-home automation. It features a sleek, modern design with a full-color touchscreen interface that makes controlling your smart home intuitive and enjoyable. The Helo Touch is part of Control4's complete home automation ecosystem, allowing you to control everything from entertainment to lighting to climate control.

Key Specifications

  • Display: Full-color touchscreen LCD
  • Design: Sleek, modern two-tone design (black screen, silver body)
  • Battery: Rechargeable lithium-ion battery
  • Charging: Docking station included
  • Range: RF (Radio Frequency) and IR (Infrared)
  • Compatibility: Works with Control4 systems
  • Interface: Touchscreen with customizable layouts

Key Features That Make the Helo Touch Stand Out

1. Beautiful Touchscreen Interface

The Helo Touch features a stunning full-color touchscreen that makes navigation intuitive and enjoyable. The interface is customizable, allowing you to organize controls exactly how you want them. Swipe, tap, and navigate your entire smart home with ease.

2. Sleek Two-Tone Design

The Helo Touch features a modern two-tone design with a dark, reflective screen on top and a brushed silver or light gray body below. This design not only looks clean but also feels great in your hand. The remote stands elegantly in its charging dock when not in use.

3. Seamless Control4 Integration

The Helo Touch doesn't just control devices - it's part of your Control4 home automation system. It communicates with Control4 controllers to coordinate multiple devices simultaneously. Press "Watch TV" and your TV turns on, lights dim, shades close, and audio switches - all automatically.

4. RF Communication

The Helo Touch uses RF (radio frequency), meaning you don't need line-of-sight. Control your home theater from the kitchen, adjust lights from the bedroom - it all works through walls. This gives you complete freedom to control your home from anywhere.

5. Extensive Device Support

The Helo Touch works with thousands of devices through your Control4 system:

  • All major TV and audio brands
  • Streaming devices (Roku, Apple TV, etc.)
  • Smart lighting (Lutron, Philips Hue, etc.)
  • Motorized shades
  • Climate control
  • Security systems
  • And much more

6. One-Button Activities

Control4's "Activities" feature lets you control multiple devices with a single tap. "Watch Movie" might turn on your projector, lower the screen, dim lights, close shades, and start your sound system - all from one button on the touchscreen.

Battery Life: What to Expect

The Helo Touch uses a rechargeable battery:

Real-World Battery Performance: With normal daily use (controlling TV, audio, lights, etc.), the Helo Touch typically lasts 5-7 days between charges. Heavy users might need to charge every 3-5 days, while light users can go 7-10 days.

Factors Affecting Battery Life:

  • RF usage: Controlling devices through walls uses more power
  • Screen brightness: Higher brightness drains battery faster (NEEO)
  • Backlight: Constant backlight reduces battery life
  • Frequency of use: More use = shorter battery life

Common Questions About the Helo Touch

Can I use the Helo Touch without a Control4 system?

No. The Helo Touch requires a Control4 controller (EA-1, EA-3, or EA-5) to function. It's designed to work as part of the Control4 ecosystem.

How long does the battery last?

The Helo Touch typically lasts 5-7 days with normal use. The touchscreen uses more power than button-based remotes, so you'll need to charge it more frequently than the SR-260.

Can I customize the touchscreen layout?

Yes! The Helo Touch interface can be customized by your Control4 dealer to match your preferences and home setup. You can organize controls exactly how you want them.

What if I lose the remote?

You can use the free Control4 app on your phone or tablet as a backup. You can also purchase additional remotes - Control4 systems support multiple remotes.

Our Verdict

The Control4 Helo Touch is an excellent choice for homeowners who want a modern touchscreen remote. It offers a beautiful interface, sleek design, and seamless integration with Control4 systems. The touchscreen makes navigation intuitive, and the customizable layouts let you organize controls your way.

However, it's not for everyone. If you prefer button-based remotes or want longer battery life, the SR-260 might be a better fit. But if you want the latest in remote control technology with a touchscreen interface, the Helo Touch is hard to beat.
