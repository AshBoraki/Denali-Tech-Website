Common questions

What homeowners ask before a Sonos project.

  • Should I use Sonos speakers or hidden speakers?

    Use visible Sonos speakers when flexibility matters. Use Sonos Amp with in-ceiling, in-wall, or outdoor speakers when you want cleaner rooms or permanent zones.

  • Why does Sonos work sometimes and fail other times?

    Many issues come from weak WiFi, poor access point placement, overloaded network gear, bad wiring, or devices spread across the wrong network paths.

  • Can Sonos be part of a theater or smart home system?

    Yes. We can keep Sonos simple on its own or tie it into TV sound, Control4, URC, lighting scenes, and outdoor entertainment areas.
