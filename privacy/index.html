Information We Collect

We collect the information you choose to send us when you fill out a form, call, email, text, or send project photos. That usually includes your name, phone number, email address, property address, city, and anything else you include about the project.

If you contact Denali Tech about an existing system, that may also include equipment details, room photos, wiring photos, screenshots, or service history you share with us.

How We Use That Information

We use contact and project information to respond, plan scope, schedule site visits, prepare proposals, coordinate installation, provide service, and support existing customers.

We may also use basic website and call-tracking data to understand which pages and lead paths are actually producing homeowner inquiries.

How We Share Information

Denali Tech does not sell your personal information. We only share it when it is needed to operate the business, support a project, or comply with the law.

That can include payment processors, form or analytics tools, manufacturers, or subcontractors only when they are directly involved in the work or support path.

Data Retention And Security

We keep project and contact information for as long as it is reasonably needed to answer your request, support your system, maintain records, or meet legal and accounting obligations.

We use practical safeguards to protect that information, but no website, inbox, or online transfer method can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure.

Your Choices

You can ask us to update your contact information, stop non-essential follow-up, or delete information that we are not required to keep for service, legal, or accounting reasons.

Send those requests to ash@denalitechs.com and include enough information for us to identify the project or contact record.

Service terms

The agreement for real work lives in the written scope

This page is not a substitute for the actual proposal or invoice. For paid work, the current written scope, schedule, and approved product list control the project.

  • Project scope, product selections, and scheduling are governed by the proposal, invoice, or written agreement tied to the work.
  • Lead times, programming time, service time, and change requests can affect schedule and final cost.
  • Manufacturer warranties and support policies remain subject to the brand involved, including Control4, URC, Lutron, networking, camera, and AV product lines.
  • Denali Tech reserves the right to decline work that depends on unsupported products, unsafe conditions, missing access, or systems we cannot reasonably stand behind.
