Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about smart home automation in Chicago - view all FAQs →

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Chicago metro area including Mt Prospect, Arlington Heights, Schaumburg, Evanston, Skokie, and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to confirm we serve your area.

How much does smart home automation cost?

Costs vary based on your home size and needs. WiFi upgrades often start around $2,000, while complete whole-home automation can range from $15,000-$50,000+. Send photos so we can tell you whether you need a focused upgrade or a full system.

What if something breaks?

Products include manufacturer warranties, and we support the install after the walkthrough. Normal first-month tweaks are expected, and WiFi coverage adjustments for agreed rooms are included for 30 days.
