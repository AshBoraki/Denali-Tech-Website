What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Chicago metro area including Mt Prospect, Arlington Heights, Schaumburg, Evanston, Skokie, and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to confirm we serve your area.

How much does smart home automation cost?

Costs vary based on your home size and needs. Simple WiFi upgrades start around $2,000, while complete whole-home automation can range from $15,000-$50,000+. Get a free quote tailored to your home.
