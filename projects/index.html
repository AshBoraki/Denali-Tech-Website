How projects usually start

Most jobs begin with a room, a symptom, or inherited equipment

  • Theater and media rooms: too many remotes, weak sound, bad wiring, or a room that never felt finished.
  • WiFi and network work: dead zones, dropped cameras, unstable streaming, or smart-home gear that keeps falling off.
  • Lighting and control: rooms that need better scenes, privacy, glare control, or cleaner whole-home flow.
  • Cameras and entries: front-door blind spots, weak awareness, or phone access that does not fit the rest of the system.
