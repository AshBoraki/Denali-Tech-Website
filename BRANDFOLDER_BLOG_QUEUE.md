## Working rules for future content
- Prefer approved product and lifestyle images from Brandfolder over generic stock.
- Use UI screenshots when explaining app or control experience.
- Use customer installation profiles when we want proof-based storytelling.
- Pair every post with a Denali Tech CTA, not just a brand feature list.
- Keep the blog about homeowner outcomes first, products second.
- Avoid copying manufacturer marketing copy word-for-word.
