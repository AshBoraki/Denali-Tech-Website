What makes the system work

Control4 works best when the network, wiring, and programming are planned together.

A stable home network supports every Control4 room, while a well-planned smart-home control system keeps lighting, AV, shades, cameras, and daily scenes working together.

  • Network stability for controllers, touchscreens, remotes, cameras, and streaming devices.
  • Clean AV routing for TVs, receivers, matrix systems, speakers, and sources.
  • Lighting and shade scenes that make sense at the wall, in the app, and from the remote.
  • Labels, walkthroughs, and support so the homeowner knows what to press.
