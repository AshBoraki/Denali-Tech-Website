Our Founder's Story: Ashkan Boraki

From Persia to the United States - A journey of passion, innovation, and making technology accessible

From a young age in Persia, Ashkan was fascinated by computers. By elementary school, he was taking apart computers and experimenting with electronics. This early passion drove him to become an entrepreneur.

In high school, Ashkan started a custom car audio shop, building innovative sound systems and solving problems. His love for technology and business grew, bringing him to the U.S. in 2013.

In America, Ashkan learned English and sharpened his programming skills, becoming a lead programmer at Magnolia Best Buy. He mastered complex AV projects and integrated cutting-edge technology into practical solutions.

In 2022, Ashkan founded Denali Tech, combining his passion for technology, programming, and home automation. Today, we use the latest technology to enhance customer experiences and make smart homes accessible to everyone.

"Ever-evolving, always learning, and dedicated to bringing the best of technology to every home."
